About ADISMUN 2023

ADISMUN 2023 is an exciting and prestigious Model United Nations (MUN) conference that brings together students, educators, and future diplomats from around the world. This year's conference promises to be an enriching experience filled with engaging debates, diplomacy, and cultural exchange.

Join us for three days of intensive committee sessions, networking opportunities, and the chance to develop your skills in negotiation, public speaking, and problem-solving. Whether you are an experienced MUN delegate or new to the world of Model UN, ADISMUN 2023 offers an inclusive and welcoming environment for all participants.
