About ADISMUN

In 2020, Abu Dhabi Indian School, Al Muroor embarked on its MUN journey with an inaugural online event, paving the way for remarkable student participation. Subsequently, the school successfully transitioned to its first physical MUN in 2022-23, and now eagerly anticipates the upcoming 2023-24 MUN edition.

Committees

Our MUN Committees offer an unparalleled opportunity for students to enhance their diplomacy, negotiation, public speaking, and critical thinking skills. As delegates, you'll delve into complex geopolitical issues, engage in rigorous debate, and work collaboratively to draft resolutions that address some of the world's most urgent problems.

Secretariat

The Secretariat is the team tasked with organizing the entire MUN. From overseeing all the staff, and coordinating with all the delegates that attend the conference, it is their leadership and contribution that is the heart and soul of the ADIS MUN. 


ADISMUN 2022

The ADIS MUN 2022, marked a significant milestone in the school's Model United Nations (MUN) journey as it proudly hosted its very first physical MUN conference.

Committees

Model United Nations (MUN) committees are at the core of MUN conferences, playing a pivotal role in shaping the experiences of students who participate in these dynamic simulations. These committees serve as the platforms where delegates, often representing different countries or stakeholders, engage in rigorous debates, negotiations, and collaborative problem-solving to address pressing global issues.
